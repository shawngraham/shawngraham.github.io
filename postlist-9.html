Welcome 2015-10-09

Welcome!

This is my Open DH Lab Notebook. I'm an associate professor of digital humanities at Carleton University.

This website, this open notebook, contains a reflection of my research notes & ephemera from my digital history and digital archaeological explorations. I update as I remember to do it. You can append rss in the address bar to get the feed. There's also an API which you may or may not find useful. And of course, all posts here are in the repo which you can fork.
