10_spearcons_-21-.md 2015-10-07

P19: 5

  • A 'spearcon' is created by speeding of speech to the point of unintelligibility but retaining pitch. '[They are] acoustic representations of spoken words

  • Research shows they work particularly well for navigating menu structures (of the kind normally found in GUI)

  • These are easier to learn than auditory icons / ear cons.
